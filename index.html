Meera's 9th Birthday

The Grimoire Mystery

✦ A Magical Murder Mystery Treasure Hunt ✦
Game Overview
18 players · 2 teams of 9 · 7 stations each · Age 9–10

Jasper the caretaker has been murdered. The Grimoire is missing. One of five suspects did it — and they're still inside the school. Two rival magical houses race through the enchanted school on different paths, gathering evidence from each crime scene. The team that completes all their stations first wins the race — but the murder cannot be solved until both teams combine their clues. Only then can the killer be named. And only Meera can open the Grimoire and reveal Jasper's final vision.
The Suspects — All were inside Wyldmoor the night Jasper was killed
🔭 Professor Orion Dusk
The Astronomy teacher. Rarely sleeps. Was seen wandering the corridors near the Grimoire vault well past midnight. Claims he was sleepwalking.
Motive: The Grimoire contains a star spell that would let him live forever. Jasper once caught him trying to sneak into the vault — and warned him never to return.
🌿 Madame Briar
The Herbology professor. Kind and gentle — or so she seems. Her prized Moonbloom plant is dying, and only a Grimoire spell can save it.
Motive: Jasper discovered she had been secretly copying pages from the Grimoire. He threatened to report her the very next morning.
🧪 Dr. Vex
The Potions master. Brilliant but chaotic. His enchanted teapot-cat is the only witness to what happened in the dungeon that night.
Motive: A potion accident turned his beloved cat into a teapot. Only the Grimoire's reversal spell can fix it — and Jasper refused to give him access.
🎭 Sister Mox
The Transfiguration teacher. She can take the shape of any person, creature, or object. On the night of the murder — nobody is sure what form she was wearing.
Motive: Jasper told the Headmistress he suspected Sister Mox of impersonating other teachers to steal exam answers. She had every reason to silence him.
📜 The Archivist
No one knows their real name or where they came from. They appeared at the school years ago and were given a room in the archive. They have read every book in the school — except the Grimoire, which Jasper personally kept locked away from them.
Motive: The Grimoire's forbidden pages contain the Archivist's true identity — a secret they will do anything to possess. Jasper was the last one standing between them and it.

⚡ The Killer — Game Master Eyes Only

The Archivist murdered Jasper. They cornered him outside the vault, forced him to reveal the password, then cast a silencing spell. The Grimoire's forbidden pages reveal that the Archivist is actually a banished dark enchanter who erased their own name from the world — and only the Grimoire can restore it. The evidence trail: ink-stained gloves at the arcade (Phoenix), a torn Grimoire page with fingerprints at the arcade (Serpent), and a signed ingredient receipt proving they acquired a silence potion the day before the murder. The Grimoire's final vision — unlocked by Meera — shows Jasper's last moments and the Archivist's face.

The Opening Story — Read aloud at the Laptop Hub
Welcome, young witches, to the Wyldmoor School of Enchantment.

Today should have been the most magical day of the year — the birthday of our most gifted student, Meera. But something terrible has happened. Something that cannot be undone with a simple spell.

This morning, at the stroke of dawn, Jasper — the school's beloved caretaker, who had looked after Wyldmoor for forty years — was found dead. He was lying in the corridor outside the Grimoire vault. The vault door was open. And the Ancient Grimoire of Wonders — the most powerful book in existence — was gone.

Jasper was the only one who knew the vault's secret password. Which means whoever killed him... made him talk first.

The school is in shock. The teachers are too frightened to investigate — because the killer is still inside these walls. It has to be one of the five people who were in the school last night. But nobody will admit to anything.

That is why you are here.

Without the Grimoire, the school's magic is dying. The enchanted halls are going dark. The potions are turning to water. The glowing stars are blinking out one by one. And poor Jasper deserves justice.

The Grimoire can only be restored by the one whose name is written on its cover. That name is Meera. But first — we must find who murdered Jasper and stole it from him.

You have been divided into two elite investigative houses. The Phoenix Order will search the northern wings. The Serpent Circle will search the southern wings. Gather your evidence. Crack every seal. The house that returns first wins the race. But the murder? The murder must be solved together.

Five suspects. One killer. Find them.

Jasper is counting on you. Your investigation begins now.

Physical Puzzle — Hidden Scroll (paper rolled up, tied with string)
Star Cipher Riddle — The scroll contains a grid of stars (★ ☆). Each filled star (★) = 1, empty star (☆) = 0. Groups of 5 stars spell binary numbers that match letters. There are 5 groups spelling a word.

The scroll reads:
★☆★☆☆ = 10100 = T
★☆★★★ = 10111 = W (given key: A=00001 through Z=11010)
(Simplified version for 9-year-olds: Give them a star-to-letter key card. Each constellation picture = one letter. There are 5 constellations hidden in the climbing area, each labelled with a star picture that matches the key card.)

Simpler version: 5 star-shaped cards are hidden along the climbing bridges. Each card has one letter on the back. Find all 5, arrange them in order of the numbers on the front (1–5).
✦ Answer / Codeword: NIGHT
🔍 Evidence found: Professor Orion's star map — with the Grimoire vault corridor circled in red ink, and a note that reads "Jasper patrols at midnight." (He knew Jasper's schedule. Very suspicious.)
🎁 Souvenir: Mini star map scroll (rolled paper with a constellation drawing)
After entering "NIGHT" on laptop
"The star map was found! Prof. Orion knew exactly when Jasper patrolled — but his telescope data may give him an alibi. File this evidence and move to your next crime scene: Herbology Grounds."

The Grimoire Chamber — Final Quest

📍 Decorated final area · Both teams · Meera's special moment

⚡ The Final Revelation

Both teams arrive and present their evidence on the laptop. Each team enters the name of their prime suspect. When both teams type THE ARCHIVIST, the laptop reveals the full accusation — the Silencing Draught, the torn Grimoire page, the signed ingredient receipt. The killer is named. Justice for Jasper.

But the Grimoire is still missing. The screen reads:

"The killer has been unmasked. But the Grimoire was hidden before the Archivist could be stopped. Jasper left one final message — written for one person only. Only Meera can read it."

The game master hands Meera a sealed envelope with her name on it. She opens it alone and reads aloud:

"Dear Meera,
I knew this day would come — so I hid the Grimoire somewhere only a true enchanter would think to look.

The school runs on magic. Magic runs on movement. Find the wall where the wheels of wonder turn and interlock — but look for the one gear that is perfectly still. That is where I left it. It has been waiting for you.

Happy Birthday, little one. The magic was always yours.
— Jasper"

Meera goes — with both teams watching and cheering — to the gear and cog wall. The Grimoire (a decorated notebook with her name on the cover) is hidden in the tray at the bottom among the loose gears. She finds it, holds it up, and everyone cheers.

She brings it back and speaks the incantation aloud:

"By the light of my birthday star,
and the magic that I am,
I open this Grimoire for Jasper's sake —
GRIMOIRE!"

She opens the book. Inside: a "Jasper's Vision" card — his final recorded memory revealing the Archivist's face. Justice is complete. Then: Wizard Investigation Certificates for every player. The school's magic is restored. Jasper is avenged. Happy Birthday, Meera.

🎁 Meera's Souvenir: Personalised Grimoire (small decorated notebook with her name on the cover)
🎁 Everyone's Souvenir: Wizard Certificate with their name, house, and the title "Investigator of Wyldmoor"

Game Master Notes
Setup (day before):
· Prepare all letter tiles, bury sand pit tiles in small ziplock bags
· Inflate and tie balloons, tape to ball pit walls at different heights
· Prepare mirror writing cards, wax rubbing cards, word search sheets
· Roll and tie star scrolls, hide on climbing structure
· Prepare the glowing sticks wall pattern (mark which sticks to check)
· Prepare the final box: does not exist anymore — instead, hide Meera's Grimoire notebook (decorated, her name on the cover) in the gear wall tray among the loose gears. Place it the morning of the party.
· Prepare a sealed envelope addressed to "Meera" containing Jasper's final letter (printed on aged/parchment paper) — give this to the game master to hand over at the finale
· Prepare a "Jasper's Vision" card to tuck inside the Grimoire notebook itself — Meera finds it when she opens the book
· Print decoder cards (24–30) — one per player
· Print clue order cards and evidence cards for each station

Teams: Phoenix Order wears red wristbands. Serpent Circle wears blue wristbands.

Laptop setup: Two identical laptops, one per team. Use a simple Google Form or Notion page where each correct codeword reveals the next clue. Or use printed "unlock envelopes" — entering the correct word allows the game master to hand over the next envelope.

Timing: Allow 5–7 minutes per station. Total game time approx. 55–65 minutes.

The race winner: The team that enters GRIMOIR at the Illumination Chamber first gets a "House Cup" trophy or ribbon before the final collaborative scene.

The accusation: Both teams must independently arrive at THE ARCHIVIST. Phoenix finds the Silencing Draught vial. Serpent finds the ingredient receipt and the torn Grimoire page. Neither team has the full picture alone — which makes the combination moment feel genuinely earned.

Meera's moment: Hand her the sealed envelope — let her read Jasper's letter aloud to everyone. Then step back and let her go find the Grimoire herself at the gear wall. Have both teams follow at a distance, cheering. When she pulls it out of the tray, that is the money shot — torch on her if possible. She walks back, speaks the incantation, opens the book, finds Jasper's Vision card inside. That is the birthday magic moment — protect it completely.
